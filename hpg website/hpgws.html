About the Game

The Hacker Prediction Game is a thrilling game where you predict the outcome of a randomly generated color and number. With a sleek hacker-themed design and glowing neon effects, this game offers an immersive experience. Place your bets, win rewards, and manage your virtual wallet!

Play the Game

How to Play

1. Select a color and a number.
2. Enter your bet amount.
3. Place your bet before the timer runs out.
4. If your prediction matches the result, you win rewards!
5. Manage your wallet to add or withdraw funds.
